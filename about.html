Our Story

Founded in 2020, Turf Booking was born out of a passion for cricket and a vision to create the perfect playing experience for enthusiasts of all levels. What started as a single turf in the heart of the city has now grown into a premier cricket facility known for its exceptional quality and service.

Our journey has been driven by a simple belief: every cricket lover deserves access to top-notch facilities that enhance their game and passion for cricket.

Our Mission

At Turf Booking, we're committed to providing:

  • World-class cricket facilities with professional-grade pitches
  • State-of-the-art equipment and amenities
  • Flexible booking options to suit every schedule
  • A safe and welcoming environment for players of all skill levels
  • Exceptional customer service that exceeds expectations
